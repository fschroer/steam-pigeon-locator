## The decided architecture (ADR-0005) — raw-primary
The EKF (`InsEkf15`) is **retired from the real-time path**; it has no live requirement once FR-P8/P9 are deferred. Real-time outputs are raw: baro (altitude + vertical velocity), GPS (position + course), IMU (g-force + rates). In-flight orientation for **air starts** (the new goal) is **safety-critical (FR-P1-class)** and comes from a **high-rate (≥ 480 Hz) strapdown gyro integrator** seeded from the on-pad vertical — *not* the EKF and *not* a complementary filter (neither fixes 20 Hz aliasing at 660–768 dps, nor heading-at-rest without a magnetometer). Gyro FS is ±4000 dps (no clip). In ballistic coast there is no gravity reference, so tilt is dead-reckoned and confidence degrades with coast time (itself an FR-P13 gate). A magnetometer is **not** on the air-start critical path.
